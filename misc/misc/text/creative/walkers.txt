
		     The Night Walkers From Flodaquoth

				    by
	
			    Brian Scott Lingard



	Over a week had passed since the walkers first appeared.  The Elders
could not explain their presence.  There was never a mention of the walkers in
the ancient prophecies.  The walkers did not speak nor did their faces show.
They wore dark brown hooded cloaks, which hung low over their faces.
