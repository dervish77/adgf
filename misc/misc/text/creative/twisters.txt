Tongue Twisters
---------------

	Muddy maniac muggers mug more muddy money. 

	Sleezy Sally sails slowly on her slinky sloop.

	Dirty dancers dance dirty dances on dirty dancing day. 

	Horrible hags haggle for honey hued harps.

	Big bouncing blue balls bounce off banded banks.

	Linn Chin wins a thin grin in winter from Ginny Lynne.
